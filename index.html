Privilege to learn with Thales Alenia Space

Thales gave me the chance to be in apprenticeship in 2012 as a mechanical part designer. Designing tool used for the assembly or test of satellite subsystem was my job. That was the opportunity to learn a lot from space context and all the domain involved.

Motivation and career evolution

After three years as a mechanical part designer, The time as came to take a big decision, continue my studies. Recruited by the R&D office : futur products, mechanisms and solar panels, I was able to be in apprenticeship as mechatronic engineer. This tree years were very interesting, I learned a lot about innovation and I was finally able to express myself in the three domains: mechanic, electronic and computing. In different use case : Prototyping, designing, controlling, testing etc.. R&D is vast domain. Also a patent is currently pending ...

Stand on your own feet

Six years in Thales Alenia Space gave me the motivation to become a freelancer. This was a really great experience to work with local companies, providing them solution in mechanic and embedded electronic. This two years experience is for me a big step forward for my autonomy and project management skills. PCB designing, microcontroller programming and mechanical part designing was the heart of this experience.

What's next ?

It's time for me to learn again. I'm looking for a company that will give me the chance to improve and learn new skills. Since I'm passionate about new technology I'm available to any propositions that include working on project that involve latest technology!
